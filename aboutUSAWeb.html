About USA Web

USAWeb is the foremost advertising and communication tool of the repossession industry.

Repossession agencies can add their company to the largest on-line directory(s) of adjusters. The basic listing is free for the first thirty days and only $100 per state for a full year. Displayed credentials and additional company information can be linked to the ad to provide a snap-shot image of the company. Ads can be enhanced to create Power Listings that rank above the basic level.

Repossession Agencies can advertise on multiple listing sites for one low price. Certain qualifications may apply.

The vendors that market their product(s) to the repossession industry have found that USAWeb sites are the place to have a presence. With advertising rates as low as $500 per year you just can't go wrong.
